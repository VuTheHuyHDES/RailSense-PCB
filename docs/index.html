Technical Specifications

Key Design Highlights

⚡

Power Management

24V Li-ion 6S3P battery. 1oz copper traces, 6.5mm power rails. Peak 11A with ΔT < 10°C. Calculated via Saturn PCB Toolkit.

IPC-2221
🛡️

Signal Integrity

Full ground plane on bottom layer. Via stitching around board edges. Complete isolation between power (motor) and signal (I2C/Encoder) zones.

EMI/EMC
🔲

Motor Control

4× BTS7960B H-Bridge drivers. PCA9685 PWM expansion via I2C. Closed-loop PID speed control with hall encoders.

BTS7960B × 4
📡

Wireless Control

ESP-NOW peer-to-peer on 802.11 PHY layer. No TCP/IP overhead. Delivery callback confirmation. Packet loss < 1%.

ESP-NOW P2P
🔊

NDT Ultrasonic

YUSHI 4MHz/20mm dual probes. Auto gel priming via 24VDC diaphragm pump. Self-locking lead screw probe deployment.

4MHz UT
📷

Computer Vision

Dual front-facing cameras for continuous rail surface imaging. Laptop-based AI processing with anti-vibration mount.

OpenCV
